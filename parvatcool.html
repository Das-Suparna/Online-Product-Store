BRAND STORY

ParvatCool began its journey in 1952, on India’s Independence Day. And over the years as India progressed, the brand also evolved in refreshing ways.

Today, it has become synonymous with the feeling of freshness as inscribed in its iconic Alive Is Awesome philosophy.

1952

A Pioneering Start

On 15th August 1952, ParvatCool began its journey as India’s first deodorant soap brand.



1989-2011

A Star - Studded Legacy

ParvatCool embraced a powerful masculine voice, perfectly captured.



2012

A New Adventure Begins

ParvatCool launched the philosophy of Alive Is Awesome that evoked the intense experience of feeling alive. This evolution fostered a fresh, new connection with its young, adventure-seeking audiences.



2013

What It Meant To Feel Alive To The Core

ParvatCool relaunched its deodorants range to inspire a young India to look inwards and feel alive to the core. Deodorants cover up the smell of body odor, usually with fragrances and block pores temporarily.



2014

ParvatCool Made The Summers Cool Again

As the summers inch closer, everyone yearns for a cool escape. So,ParvatCool launched a dedicated campaign for its Cool variant. Needless to say, everyone had an icy cool blast while keeping their skin summer-proof.



2015

When Awesome Adventures Went Offline


When staying alive became synonymous with staying online, ParvatCool encouraged its audience to go offline and discover the truly alive feeling. A simple reminder that the real world is far more rewarding than the world wide web.



2016

The Year Freshness Was Reborn

It was the year when the deo was reborn as ParvatCool Deo Stick-A unique cream-based formula that revolutionised the way people carried the alive fragrance.

2017

Next, ParvatCool Challenged The Sun

ParvatCool’s #SunKoKarDoSet campaign enabled adventurers to challenge the unforgiving Sun with ParvatCool Cool.




2018

It Was Time To #WakeUpAlive

With the #WakeUpAlive campaign, ParvatCool Lime promised to give you a fresh lemony start to the day. A promise that it has kept since then.




2021

When The World Needed A Fresh Restart

As the world slowly got back on its feet, everyone needed to feel alive again. Keeping this in mind, the brand took the audience on a surreal adventure to experience the lemony freshness of ParvatCool Lime & the icy-cool blast of ParvatCool Cool.
